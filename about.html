About me

I recently completed my studies to become a front-end developer at Noroff School of Technology, graduating in the summer of 2024 from their two-year program. Throughout the course, I learned essential coding languages and gained hands-on experience with UI/UX design. I particularly enjoy front-end development because it allows me to solve problems through code while expressing creativity through design.

Prior to my studies, I worked as a hairdresser for seven years and continue to work part-time in the field. Although this experience is quite different from my current career path, it has equipped me with valuable skills and a strong work ethic.

Combining my background in hairdressing with my passion for technology provides a unique perspective to my work as a front-end developer. I tackle challenges with enthusiasm and dedication, consistently seeking innovative solutions and striving to create user-friendly experiences.
