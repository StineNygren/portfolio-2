About me

I'm currently studying to become a front-end developer at Noroff School of Technology. It's a two-year program, and I'll be graduating by summer 2024. During this course, I'll learn all the important coding languages and also get hands-on experience with UI/UX design. What I enjoy most about front-end development is that it lets me work on problem-solving in my code while also being visually creative with the design.

Before becoming a student, I worked as a hairdresser for seven years, and I still work part-time in that field while studying. Although it's quite different from what I'm learning now, I've gained valuable skills and developed a strong work ethic during my time as a hairdresser and from previous work.

I believe that merging my background in hairdressing with my passion for technology brings a unique perspective to my work as a front-end developer. I approach challenges with enthusiasm and dedication, always striving to find innovative solutions and create user-friendly experiences.
